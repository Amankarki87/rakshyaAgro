About Us

Rakshya Agro Industries,

Rakshya Agro Industries Pvt. Ltd., distinguishes itself as a comprehensive and pioneering force within the agricultural landscape, driven by an unwavering commitment to deliver an extensive spectrum of superior-quality products and services that cater to diverse needs. Our involvement spans a multitude of critical agricultural domains, commencing with our sustainable poultry division. Here, we prioritize the well-being of our poultry through humane farming practices, ensuring a healthy and stress-free environment that translates directly into the exceptional quality and nutritional value of our poultry products. Complementing this is our state-of-the-art, ethically managed slaughtering facilities, where stringent hygiene protocols and humane handling techniques are paramount, guaranteeing the freshest and most wholesome poultry meat for consumers.

 

Building upon our expertise in meat processing, Rakshya Agro Industries has established robust capabilities in the efficient processing and distribution of premium red meats. Mirroring our poultry operations, our red meat division adheres to the highest ethical standards throughout the entire supply chain, from sourcing to processing, ensuring that only the finest quality meats reach our customers. We understand the critical role of preservation in the agricultural sector, and to this end, we have invested significantly in developing and maintaining cutting-edge cold storage infrastructure. These advanced facilities provide optimal temperature and humidity control, effectively extending the shelf life and preserving the inherent freshness and quality of both our processed meats and the diverse range of crops we handle. This capability is crucial in minimizing post-harvest losses and ensuring a consistent supply of nutritious food.

 

Beyond animal husbandry, Rakshya Agro Industries is deeply engaged in crop harvesting. We actively cultivate strategic partnerships with local farmers and deploy modern agricultural technologies and sustainable farming methodologies to optimize crop yields while minimizing environmental impact. Our harvesting operations encompass a variety of essential crops, contributing significantly to regional food security and supporting the livelihoods of agricultural communities. We are committed to continuous improvement and innovation across all facets of our operations, constantly exploring new technologies and sustainable practices to enhance efficiency, reduce our environmental footprint, and further elevate the quality of our offerings. At Rakshya Agro Industries, our core values revolve around a deep respect for animal welfare, unwavering operational integrity, and an unwavering dedication to achieving and exceeding customer satisfaction. We strive to be more than just an agricultural company; we aim to be a trusted partner in the food supply chain, contributing to a sustainable and secure agricultural future for all stakeholders. Our holistic approach, encompassing everything from ethical animal rearing and processing to advanced crop management and preservation, positions Rakshya Agro Industries as a truly comprehensive and dependable provider in the dynamic and essential agro-industry.

 

Guided by a commitment to quality, affordability, and sustainability, Saurya Krishi Company Limited is poised to play a vital role in Nepal's agricultural modernization while contributing significantly to the nation's economic development.
