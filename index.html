Message From Chairman

Welcome to Rakshya Agro Industries. It is with immense pride and vision that I address you. Our journey is rooted in a profound commitment to sustainable and ethical agriculture, reflecting our deep respect for animal welfare, operational integrity, and unwavering dedication to customer satisfaction.

At Rakshya Agro Industries, we see ourselves as more than just an agricultural company; we are a trusted partner in the food supply chain. Our holistic approach spans ethical animal rearing and processing to advanced crop management and preservation, ensuring the highest quality products for our valued customers.

 

Our ongoing projects and commitments underscore our dedication to innovative technologies and sustainable practices, aiming to enhance efficiency and reduce our environmental footprint. With our expansion into buffalo farming and processing within an FMD-free zone, we are poised to serve both domestic and international markets, further solidifying our role in ensuring food security and supporting agricultural communities.

We are committed to continuous improvement and strive to contribute to a sustainable and secure agricultural future for all stakeholders. Thank you for your interest in Rakshya Agro Industries. We look forward to building a healthier, more sustainable future together.

Yuktarth Aryal

Chairman
