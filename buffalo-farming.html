Buffalo Farming & Slaughtering Expansion

Poultry Farming

Rakshya Agro Industries is expanding its operations to include buffalo farming, along with establishing a specialized slaughterhouse for buffalo processing. This expansion involves thermal processing at 70 degrees Celsius and is strategically located in a Foot-and-Mouth Disease (FMD) free zone as mandated by the Government of Nepal. The company plans to export buffalo meat and its by-products to international markets.

Buffalo Farming Operations

Rakshya Agro Industries is incorporating buffalo farming into its existing agricultural activities.

Location and Compliance:

The slaughterhouse will be located within an FMD-free zone, adhering to the regulatory requirements of the Government of Nepal.

Export Strategy:

The company aims to export buffalo meat and its by-products to foreign countries.
